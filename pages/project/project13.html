AR Car Visualizer

COMPLETED ON: 10.AUGUST.2020

Skills Involved: Unity C# Augmented Reality Simulation

Mentor: Himanshu Gupta

Links:

The Unity Engine and Google ARCore API were used to create this AR Car Visualization. Google's augmented reality platform, ARCore, allows developers to create augmented reality experiences. Plane detection and In-App Customization Menus are among the features available in this app.

This app gives users the option of selecting one of three cars and changing the color and orientation. The plane in which the appears can be chosen by the user. Multiple cars of various colors and types can be spawned.
